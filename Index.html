Our FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Problem statement: Facebook affects the behavior of grade 10 APEC Sta. Rita Students

Question Answer
What broadcasting software will we use? We chose OBS Studio, because it is user-friendly, it is free, and is easily manageable by low end to high end computers.
Why are students' behavior changing to Facebook? Students or teenagers are more easily led by the things they see than adults.
Why are students/teenagers more influenced by Facebook? As we've said in the previous question, it is because they are easily influenced by the things they see and are more gullible than adults. We can control this by studying their changes and spreading awareness using things such as podcasts.
Why should we use podcasts to spread awareness? Podcasts are easily accessible to the public and so many of the population listen to them on the daily.
How are podcasts easily accessible to the public? You can listen to podcasts for free and anytime, anywhere. You can listen to podcasts using Spotify, Youtube, and many more streaming platforms.
Why are podcasts helpful for us to share information about the effects of Facebook? Because as streamers, podcasts will help us have the flexibility to cover topics that require a long form and help our audience explore further and give them ways to learn more plus it is easier and cost-effective than video streaming.
